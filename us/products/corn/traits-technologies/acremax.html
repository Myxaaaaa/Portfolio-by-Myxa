      Optimum® Acremax® Family

      Locally tested, high-yielding corn products in single-bag refuge solutions for the Corn Belt.

      Jump to:

      Your Insect-Protection Solution in a Single Bag

      The Pioneer® brand Optimum® AcreMax® family of products delivers locally tested hybrids with high yield potential in single-bag refuge solutions. Use the multiple modes of action for enhanced defense against pests.

      Optimum® AcreMax® Insect Protection

      Optimum® AcreMax® insect protection provides a single-bag, multiple modes of action solution for above-ground insect protection. It contains 95 percent of a Pioneer® brand hybrid with two modes of action above ground insect protection (HX1, YGCB) blended with 5 percent in-the-bag refuge of a similar non-Bt hybrid with herbicide tolerance (RR2 or RR2/LL) to meet corn borer refuge requirements.

      Optimum® AcreMax® 1 Insect Protection

      Optimum® AcreMax® 1 insect protection was the industry’s first in-the-bag solution for corn rootworm refuge. This solution eliminates the challenges associated with planting an in-field or adjacent corn rootworm refuge. Each product contains 90 percent of a Pioneer® brand hybrid with Herculex® XTRA (CRW/CB/LL/RR2) insect protection blended with 10 percent of a similar Pioneer hybrid with the Herculex I insect protection trait (CB/LL/RR2), which serves as the corn rootworm refuge in the seed bag. The 20 percent structured corn borer refuge may be planted up to ½ mile away. This helps you maximize field-by-field productivity and increase yield potential in fields with corn rootworm pressure by placing in-plant protection where needed.

      Optimum® AcreMax® Xtra Insect Protection

      Satisfy all your above- and below-ground refuge requirements with Optimum® AcreMax® Xtra insect protection. Each in-the-bag product contains a Pioneer® brand hybrid with three trusted and industry-leading traits (HXX, YGCB) blended with 5 percent in-the-bag refuge of a similar non-Bt hybrid with herbicide tolerance (RR2 or RR2/LL) which satisfies all corn borer and corn rootworm refuge requirements in the Corn Belt. This helps you maximize corn yield potential while preserving valuable in-plant insect protection for the future.

      Optimum® AcreMax® XTreme Insect Protection

      With multiple modes of action, Optimum® AcreMax® XTreme insect protection delivers enhanced above- and below-ground insect control and simplified refuge management on your acres. Each product contains 95 percent of a Pioneer® brand hybrid two modes of action for above- and two modes of action for below-ground insect protection (HXX, YGCB, RW) blended with 5 percent in-the-bag refuge of a similar non-Bt hybrid with herbicide tolerance (RR2 or RR2/LL). This solution provides simplicity and choice to maximize your yields.

      Optimum® AcreMax® Leptra® products

      Optimum® AcreMax® Leptra® products provide strong above-ground insect control with a superior level of efficacy against ear-feeding pests for cleaner ears and improved grain quality. These products provide protection against a broad spectrum of pests, including corn earworm, European corn borer, southwestern corn borer, fall armyworm, black cutworm and western bean cutworm. Each product contains 95 percent of a Pioneer® brand hybrid with three modes of above-ground insect protection (HX1, YGCB, AVBL), blended with 5 percent in-the-bag refuge of a similar non-Bt hybrid with herbicide tolerance (RR2 or RR2/LL). A 20 percent separate structured refuge is required in EPA-designated cotton counties.
